Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Mentova Academy Inc. ("we", "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website mentova-academy.com and use our mobile application.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, login status, and browsing activity. Cookies can be "session" cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent" cookies (remain until they expire or you delete them).

2. Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly. These include session cookies for authentication, form submissions, and security. You cannot opt out of these.
  • Preference Cookies: Store your language preference (FR, EN, ES), theme settings, and dismissed banners (e.g., announcement bar). These improve your experience.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site. We use these to improve our content and user experience. Data is anonymized and aggregated.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies, including: - Netlify (hosting and performance optimization) - Stripe (payment processing, when applicable) These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to: - View and delete individual cookies - Block third-party cookies - Block cookies from specific sites - Block all cookies - Delete all cookies when you close your browser Note: Disabling essential cookies may prevent certain features from working properly.

5. Local Storage

In addition to cookies, we may use browser local storage to save user preferences (language selection, dismissed notifications). Local storage functions similarly to cookies but can store more data and is only accessible by our website.

6. Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of our site after changes constitutes acceptance.
